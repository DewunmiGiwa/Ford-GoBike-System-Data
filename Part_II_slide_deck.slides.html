Part II - (Ford GoBike System Data Explanatory Visualization)¶

by (Adewunmi Giwa)¶

Investigation Overview¶

I will be investigating the usage of Ford Go Bike. Specifically, I intend to find out when they mostly used in terms of time of day, day of week, and month of year; the average trip distance and trip duration; whether most users are subscribers or customers; and whether the findings from above depend on whether the user is a customer or subscriber.

Dataset Overview¶

This data set includes information about individual rides made in a bike-sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area. I obtained links to monthly datasets from January to December, 2019 from this lnk: https://s3.amazonaws.com/baywheels-data/index.html

I programatically downloaded the zipfiles containing the dataset for each month using os and requests libraries. Thereafter, I extracted the dataset from each file. I then merged all monthly datasets into one single csv fle.
